About Us

The Project Archive is a creative studio based in the Maldives, dedicated to capturing life’s most meaningful moments. Founded in 2024 from a passion for storytelling, we’ve since grown into a full-service studio transforming weddings, events, and everyday stories into timeless keepsakes. We don’t just document scenes; we capture the laughter between the poses, the tears during vows, the energy on the dance floor, and the quiet moments in between—preserving memories that last a lifetime.
